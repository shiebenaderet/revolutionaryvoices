Basic Information Step 1

What makes a good name: Short, memorable, and hints at your topic. Try powerful words like “Untold,” “Hidden,” or “Chronicles.”
Examples:
  • “Voices of Resistance” — about people fighting back
  • “Hidden Patriots” — creates mystery and intrigue
  • “Untold Stories 1776” — specific and attention-grabbing
Why these work: short, memorable, and they tell listeners what to expect.
